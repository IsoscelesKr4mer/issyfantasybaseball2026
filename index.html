📚 League Narratives

The Waiver Wire Wins Championships. In 2025, the top 4 finishers averaged 85 moves. The bottom 4 averaged 52. LetsPlay won with 81 moves; Rain City finished 9th with just 17. Good Vibes Only made the most moves (101) and finished 4th despite the 6th-best draft. The lesson is clear: ~30% of your Opening Day roster will be gone by June. Draft for flexibility, not perfection.

The Champion's Blueprint. LetsPlay won 2025 by pairing Elly De La Cruz's SB dominance with 3 closers and aggressive waiver activity. In 2026, they doubled down — drafting Elly again at pick 4, loading up on 3 closers (Iglesias, Estévez, Hader), and adding the same Joe Ryan and Luzardo they rode in 2025. This is a team that knows its identity.

One Ball's Model Season. Picked 8th in 2025 (same slot as Buckner Boots in 2026). Took Elly De La Cruz, paired with Mookie Betts and Devers, and rode waiver activity (63 moves) to 2nd place at 117-76-17. The 2026 Buckner Boots studied this blueprint — elite batter at #8 (Tucker), OBP depth, and pitching volume over aces.
