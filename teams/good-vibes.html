👤 Team Profile

Fourth place. Not bad, not great. Good Vibes Only is a team name that either reflects a genuine management philosophy or a coping mechanism deployed after a tough categories loss — hard to say which without the therapy notes.

Michael clearly believes in positive energy, deep pitching staffs, and trusting the process. The 6th pick is respectable. If the rotation depth holds and the bats produce, this team has a legitimate top-4 ceiling. If the vibes go bad — and they will, at some point around Week 11 when three SPs hit the IL simultaneously — expect a notable ERA week.

The upside here is real and the floor is respectable. Good Vibes only makes it to October if the vibes stay mostly good, which in fantasy baseball is entirely outside your control.
