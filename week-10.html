📚 Storylines to Watch

Allahu Alvarez gets the test. After the best weekly all-play of the season (8–0–1, 53 cat wins, 1.16 ERA, .88 WHIP), TylerR draws Keanu Reeves, the cumulative #2. Sim has Keanu 52%. Yordan and Max Muncy are both DTD and the Allahu rotation gets a 5–0 plus 14 HR run for the second straight week or this is the matchup that calls it a fluke. Best test on the board for the 8th-place team that just looked elite.

Sonny rotation gets the deep-end test. Cumulative #1 vs #10. Sonny (cumulative 442 cats) draws Rain City (cumulative 322 cats). The interesting wrinkle: Rain City is now rolling Skenes, deGrom, Wheeler, Imanaga, Lodolo as a top-end rotation while Sonny is missing Cole Ragans, Woodruff, and Jared Jones from theirs. Sim still says 69% Sonny because the lineup gap is real, but the pitching matchup is closer than the record suggests.

Ete Crow's bounce-back call. 0–8–1 all-play to ending the week 7th in the standings. Jace gets Nick-fil-A as the 75% favorite, same structural matchup that ended in disaster last week (62% favored, tied). Vladimir Guerrero Jr. should be back from DTD. Hunter Greene, Glasnow, Helsley all out for Nick. If the offense disappears against a depleted Nick rotation, the storyline shifts from variance to chronic.

OBT–Buckner is the rotation depth showdown. Two teams with active SP rotations averaging 4 healthy starters apiece. Sim has Buckner 75% on lineup edges (Yandy, Murakami, Henderson, Tucker) but OBT has the closer corps (Jansen, Bednar, Megill, 83% SV edge). The K cat is essentially even. This is the matchup where the lineups decide everything; if Garth’s offense outscores expectation, the sim model gets to 0–4 versus OBT.

Romance Explosion has to remember how to hit. Same lineup that went 8–1 in Week 8 went 0–6–3 in Week 9. Michael 1 dropped Buehler and the Hey Holmes! brand name in the same week. Now he draws Busch Latte at 30%. Both teams are 29–46–5 in the standings. Busch has the depth, Romance has the higher ceiling. Variance is variance until it happens twice.
