🔎 Week 1 Preview — Mar 25–Mar 29

📚 Storylines to Watch

Good Vibes Only is running a fantasy MASH unit and it’s only Opening Day. Burnes [IL60], Hunter Greene [IL60], and Schwellenbach [IL60] are gone until late May — and that’s before you count Bieber [IL15], Pepiot [IL15], and Musgrove [DTD]. The important nuance: those IL60 guys occupy IL slots, so the active roster can still field Crochet, Cease, Bradish, and Gore. They’re not down six pitchers. They’re just down six good ones. The offense — Harper, Betts, Alonso, Rodríguez — will carry this team further than the rotation deserves.

Rain City burned three IL slots before the first pitch. Wheeler [IL15], Lodolo [IL15], and Merrill Kelly [IL15] are all parked. The silver lining is those are IL slots, not active roster holes — Skenes, deGrom, Imanaga, and Sasaki are all still available and healthy. Lindor going [DTD] is the one that actually stings because it hits both run scoring and SB. Cleavinger was added today as RP depth but there’s no real closer anchor here. The ceiling is intact; the floor got lower.

Skenes’n on deez Hoerners arrived to Opening Day with a shopping list. Four moves in one morning: Cade Cavalli, Logan Henderson [NA], Jordan Westburg [IL10], and Jackson Holliday [IL10]. The Westburg and Holliday stashes are savvy IL management — high-upside players with a return timeline that fits within the next month, parked while the active roster stays full. They drafted last, assembled the most intimidating batting order in the league, and immediately started playing the long game. Filed under: this team knows what they’re doing.

Week 1 is only five days. Fewer SP starts, fewer save chances, fewer plate appearances across your lineup. The short week amplifies closer depth: Decoy’s Miller + Helsley and One Ball’s Bednar + Jansen get a relative edge because the SV pool is just smaller. It also means Good Vibes’ rotation woes are slightly less catastrophic than they’d be in a full 7-day week. Start your best arms, don’t stream junk to fill slots, and don’t read too much into a 5-day sample. Except for Good Vibes. Read everything into their pitching situation. It’s bad.
